How FreshSense Works

FreshSense combines a smart odor sensor with an easy‑to‑use mobile app, turning early smell changes into clear actions for farmers.

  • Place the compact odor sensor in your mushroom grow room—no water or complex setup needed.
  • The sensor continuously reads the air for hidden signs of mold, bad bacteria, or substrate issues.
  • The app shows a simple green‑to‑warning status and sends instant alerts when something changes.

A Mobile App for Farmers

The FreshSense app shows exactly what each sensor is detecting. When the air is clean and healthy, everything appears green. If something unusual starts to happen, the app changes color and sends clear messages such as:

  • “Warning: Possible mold activity in Room 2.”
  • “Alert: Air quality changing—check humidity or ventilation.”

Farmers can then act quickly by adjusting temperature, cleaning trays, improving airflow, or removing infected spots. The interface is designed to be easy to understand so any farmer, no matter their background, can use it comfortably.
